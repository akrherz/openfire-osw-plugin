The OneSocialWeb project team is not currently driving active development of the source code. But we continue our work on specifications and standards to make the dream of a world where all social networks are connected and work together in a way similar to email a reality. This project aimed at defining a language to bridge these networks and make it easy for social networks to join a bigger social web.
